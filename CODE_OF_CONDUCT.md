# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

PtheusLab is committed to a welcoming and professional environment for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

**Expected behavior:**
- Communicate respectfully and constructively
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive conduct
- Publishing others' private information without consent

---

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to **conduct@ptheus.cc.cd** (or via GitHub's private reporting). All reports are handled confidentially.

Consequences range from a warning to a permanent ban, depending on severity and context.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all PtheusLab spaces — repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other official channels.
